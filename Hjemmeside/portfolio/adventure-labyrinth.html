Adventure labyrinth - An exhibit

For my first project for a client, I was tasked with creating a prototype
of an exhibit that was going to be placed in the H.C. Andersen museum
in Odense. My group and I realized that the museum was lacking a bit
in exhibits aimed at children, so we decided to construct a maze where
you get led through some of Andersen's most famous adventures. A full
size model was never built, but here is some of the material we ended up presenting.
