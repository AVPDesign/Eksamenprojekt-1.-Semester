The old guitarist - Animation

Part of a "moving art" workshop, I was tasked with finding
a painting and, well, as you can see, animating it! I chose
"The old guitarist", an oil painting by Pablo Picasso, and after
some work in Photoshop and Animate, this is the final result!
If you wait a little , you might even get to hear him play a bit.
